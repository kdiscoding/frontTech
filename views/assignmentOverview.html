Assignment 1: Introduction to Data Science

This assignment is designed to assess your understanding of the fundamental concepts covered in the first module of our Introduction to Psychology course. It will test your knowledge on the history of psychology, major perspectives, and research methods. Please read the instructions carefully and ensure you address all aspects of the assignment thoroughly.

Instructions:
1. Read the assigned chapters in the textbook and review the lecture
slides.
2. Answer the following questions in a well-structured essay format.
3. Ensure your essay is clear, concise, and supported by evidence from the course materials.
4. Cite any external sources using APA style.
Questions:
1. Briefly describe the historical development of psychology, highlighting key figures and their contributions.
2. Compare and contrast the major perspectives in psychology (e.g., biological, cognitive, behavioral, psychodynamic, humanistic).
